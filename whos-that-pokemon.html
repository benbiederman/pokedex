Who's That Pokemon?

Instructions:

1. Once you click 'Start' a silhouette of a pokemon will appear. Based on the silhouette, it's your job to identify which Pokemon it is.

2. Type your guess - capitalization doesn't matter, but spelling does! No cheating!

3. Submit your guess and find out if you're correct.
